Sweet Potatoes (Crock Pot)

 serves 4-6

  • 4-6 medium Sweet Potatoes ; peeled + sliced      
  • 1 cup Brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1-2 tbsp Butter 
  1. Lightly butter the slow cooker
  2. Add sliced sweet potatoes + sprinkle with salt
  3. Combine sugar, cornstarch + cinnamon
  4. Sprinkle over sweet potatoes
  5. Dot with butter
  6. Cover and cook on low for 7-9 hours

Chicken Parmingiana

serves 4

  • 3 Chicken breasts - skinned + deboned 
  • 1 Egg beaten 
  • 1 cup Bread crumbs 
  • 1 tbsp Margarine 
  • 1 jar (28oz) Spaghetti sauce 
  • 1/2 cups Water
  • 3-4 slices Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cups grated Parmesan
  • Noodles 
  1. Dip chicken in egg the bread crumbs
  2. Brown in Margarine
  3. Place in baking dish
  4. Pour on 1/2 sauce
  5. Cover with mozzarella 
  6. Pour on remaining sauce + sprinkle with parmesan
  7. Bake 350 degrees for 45 mins
  8. Serve over noodles

Zucchini Bread

serves 8-10

  • 2 cups shifted All purpose flour 
  • 2 tsp Baking soda 
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Baking powder
  • 3 tsp ground Cinnamon
  • 3 Eggs 
  • 1 cup Vegetable oil  
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 2 cups grated Zucchini 
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 cup Chocolate chips 
  1. Sift flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder and cinnamon onto waxed paper
  2. Combine eggs, oil, suga, zucchini and vanill. Beat until well mixed
  3.  Stir in flour mixture until smooth. Stir in chocolate chip
  4. Pour into greased 13x9x2" pan  
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes

Apple Pie

serves 8 

  • Crust
  • 6-7 medium Apples 
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup Sugar 
  • 2 tbsp Cornstarch 
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg 
  • 1/3 cup Corn syrup
  •  1 tbsp Lemon juice
  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Perpare pastry 
  3. Slice apples into pastry shell
  4. Combine remaining ingredients + pour over apples
  5. Cover with crust 
  6. Seal edge
  7. Bake 450 degrees for 15 minutes 
  8. Reduce heat to 375 degrees + continue to bake  30 minutes longer
